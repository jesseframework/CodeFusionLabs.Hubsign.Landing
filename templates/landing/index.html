Document Manager

More than e-signatures.
A complete DMS.

HubSign's built-in Document Management System gives your team a secure, structured home for every file — whether it's been signed or not. Organise, classify, retrieve and audit documents at enterprise scale.

  • Filing Structure

    Organise documents into nested cabinets, folders, and sub-folders with customisable locations.

  • Full-Text Search & Retrieval

    OCR-powered indexing lets you find any document by content, tag, date, or classification instantly.

  • Audit Trail & Version History

    Every view, edit, check-out, and signature is logged with timestamp and user identity.

  • Classification & Tags

    Apply document types, confidentiality levels, and custom tags for precise governance.

  • Check-out / Check-in

    Lock documents for exclusive editing and prevent conflicting changes in collaborative workflows.

  • OCR Processing

    Scanned PDFs and images are automatically processed so their content is fully searchable.
